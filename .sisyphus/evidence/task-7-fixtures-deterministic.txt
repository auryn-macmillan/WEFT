Task T7 — Determinism Evidence
==============================

Date: 2026-04-22

Test: two consecutive runs of fixture-gen binary produce bit-identical output.

Command:
  ./target/release/fixture-gen /tmp/fx1
  ./target/release/fixture-gen /tmp/fx2
  diff -r /tmp/fx1 /tmp/fx2

Result: no diff output — runs are bit-identical.

Root cause of prior non-determinism:
  fhe.rs PublicKey::new and SecretKey::try_encrypt both call thread_rng() internally
  to seed the `a` polynomial, bypassing any caller-provided RNG.

Fix applied:
  Replaced PublicKey-based encryption with SecretKey::try_encrypt_with_seed(pt, seed, rng).
  The `seed` ([u8; 32]) is drawn from ChaCha20Rng::from_seed([42u8; 32]) before each
  encrypt call, making the entire generation pipeline fully deterministic.
